Ingredients
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup milk
Preparation
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix dry ingredients together in medium mixing bowl
  3. Stir in wet ingredients until blended thoroughly
  4. Bake for 1 hour
Notes
Exact cooking time may vary.
Margarine may be substituted for butter.
